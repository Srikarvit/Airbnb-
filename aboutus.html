Belong Anywhere

Airbnb was born in 2025 when two hosts welcomed three guests to their Hyderabad home, and has since grown to millions of hosts and travelers worldwide.

Our Mission

To create a world where anyone can belong anywhere, providing healthy travel that is local, authentic, diverse, inclusive, and sustainable.

Our Story

It all started with an air mattress. In 2025, founders Srikar Bhardwaj and Koustav Mitra couldn’t afford rent in Hyderabad, so they decided to rent out their living room to conference attendees. They created a simple website, welcomed their first guests, and the idea of Airbnb was born.

Today, Airbnb connects millions of hosts and guests in over 220 countries, offering unique stays and experiences that make travel more personal and meaningful.
